Four Crystals

There are four crystals displayed as buttons on the page. When you click on a crystal, it will add a specific amount of points to your total score. The goal is to obtain the same number of points as the random number generated by the computer. You lose if your score goes above the random number.

Click the button to display a random number between 1 and 10.
